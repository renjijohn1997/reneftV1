Services

HVAC Design

Smart systems. Comfortable spaces. We design high-performance HVAC systems for residential and commercial buildings, guided by internationally recognized standards like ASHRAE and CIBSE.

  • Heat and cooling load calculations
  • Psychrometric and moisture analysis
  • Ventilation and fresh air strategies
  • Equipment sizing and selection
  • Energy efficiency optimization

MEP Drafting Services

Clear drawings. Confident execution. We provide detailed 2D and 3D drafting for Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) systems.

  • Coordination drawings for clash-free installations
  • As-fitted drawings based on onsite verification
  • Revit and AutoCAD drafting
  • Support for consultants, contractors, and facility managers

CFD Analysis

See the air. Solve the problem. Our Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations help visualize and optimize airflow in complex environments.

  • Ventilation analysis for enclosed car parks
  • Thermal comfort studies for indoor spaces
  • Airflow modeling in swimming pools and spas
  • Data center cooling performance
  • Smoke and contaminant dispersion analysis
